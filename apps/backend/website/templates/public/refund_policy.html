Refund Policy

This Refund Policy explains how billing reversals, cancellations, and refund requests are handled for Work Zilla services.

1. Policy Scope

This policy applies to paid Work Zilla SaaS subscriptions, plan renewals, and add-on purchases completed through approved payment channels.

2. Trial and Evaluation

Where trial access is available, customers should evaluate the platform during the trial period before purchasing. Trial periods are intended to reduce refund disputes by enabling informed purchase decisions.

3. Monthly Subscription Charges

Monthly plan fees are generally non-refundable after billing is successfully processed and service access is granted. Cancellation stops future cycles and does not automatically reverse the current paid cycle.

4. Yearly Subscription Charges

Annual plans are generally non-refundable once activated, except where refund is required by applicable law or approved by Work Zilla under exceptional circumstances.

5. Add-ons and One-Time Charges

Add-on user packs, setup charges, and one-time commercial items are non-refundable after successful activation, unless charged in error.

6. Duplicate or Incorrect Charges

If duplicate billing or an incorrect charge occurs due to technical or processing error, customers should report it promptly. Verified billing errors are eligible for correction or refund.

7. Chargebacks and Payment Disputes

Please contact Work Zilla support before initiating a chargeback. Unauthorized or abusive disputes may result in temporary service restrictions while the case is reviewed.

8. Cancellation and Future Billing

You may request cancellation to prevent future renewal charges. Cancellation timing and entitlement follow your active plan cycle and contract terms.

9. Refund Request Process

  • Submit request through our Contact Information page.
  • Include billing email, transaction details, and reason for request.
  • Our team reviews requests and shares decision and next steps.

10. Processing Timeline

Approved refunds are initiated promptly. Final credit timeline depends on payment gateway, card issuer, bank policies, and settlement systems.
