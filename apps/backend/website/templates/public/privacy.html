Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Work Zilla collects, uses, stores, and protects personal and business data when you use our websites and SaaS products.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account information: name, company name, email, phone number, login details.
  • Billing information: invoicing profile, plan details, transaction metadata.
  • Usage information: product actions, audit logs, performance and diagnostic events.
  • Support information: enquiry forms, ticket messages, and attachments you submit.

2. How We Use Information

  • To provide, operate, and improve Work Zilla services.
  • To manage subscriptions, renewals, invoicing, and payment support.
  • To secure accounts, prevent abuse, and enforce platform policies.
  • To respond to support, legal, and compliance requests.

3. Monitoring and Workplace Data

Some Work Zilla products include workforce activity visibility features. Customers are responsible for lawful implementation in their organization, including employee notice, consent requirements, and local labor/privacy compliance.

4. Legal Basis and Processing Responsibility

Depending on use case and jurisdiction, processing may be based on contract performance, legitimate interests, legal obligations, or consent. In many business scenarios, the customer organization acts as data controller and Work Zilla acts as service provider/processor.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. We may share data with trusted infrastructure, analytics, communication, and payment partners only to operate the service, under appropriate security and confidentiality controls.

6. Data Security

We apply reasonable technical and organizational safeguards, such as access controls, auditability, and secure application practices. No internet-based system can be guaranteed as 100% secure.

7. Retention

We retain data only for business, contractual, legal, and support needs. Retention periods may vary by product module, customer settings, and legal obligations.

8. Cookies and Similar Technologies

We may use cookies or similar mechanisms for authentication, session continuity, security, and site performance.

9. Your Rights

Subject to applicable law, you may request access, correction, or deletion of eligible personal information. For organization-managed accounts, requests may need to be coordinated through your organization administrator.

10. Policy Updates

We may update this policy from time to time. Material updates will be reflected with a revised “Last updated” date.

11. Contact Information

For privacy or compliance questions, please visit our Contact Information page.
